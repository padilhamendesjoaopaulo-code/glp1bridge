    What "qualifying condition" means

    Medicare Part D coverage of GLP-1 medications has historically required a recognized medical indication rather than weight loss alone. Type 2 diabetes is the most established, and the FDA has approved certain GLP-1s (such as Wegovy) for reducing cardiovascular risk in some patients. Obesity-only coverage is the newest and most plan-dependent area. See our covered drugs comparison and FAQ for details.
