Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information GLP1Bridge.com ("we," "us," or "our") collects when you visit our website, how that information is used, and the choices you have. By using the site, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

You do not need an account to use GLP1Bridge.com, and our interactive tools — the eligibility wizard, cost calculator, and weight-projection calculator — run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store the answers you enter into these tools.

Like most websites, we and our service providers automatically collect limited, non-identifying technical information when you visit, which may include:

  • Device and browser data — such as browser type, operating system, and screen size.
  • Usage data — pages you view, links you click, and the approximate region you connect from (derived from your IP address).
  • Cookies and similar technologies — small files stored on your device, including the advertising and analytics cookies and advertising identifiers described below.

If you contact us by email, we receive whatever information you choose to include in your message.

Cookies and advertising (Google AdSense)

We use third-party advertising to support the site, including Google AdSense. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies — such as the DoubleClick DART cookie — and advertising identifiers to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites across the Internet.

  • Google and its partners use advertising cookies and identifiers to serve and personalize the ads you see.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You can learn how Google uses information from sites that use its services on Google's "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services" page.

Third-party analytics

We may use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to understand how visitors use the site — for example, which pages are read most often. These services use cookies and report aggregated, non-identifying usage data. We do not use analytics to personally identify you.

Your choices and opt-out options

You are in control of advertising and analytics cookies:

  • Personalized ads from Google: manage or turn off personalized advertising in Google Ads Settings.
  • Other ad vendors: opt out of personalized advertising from many companies at optout.aboutads.info (Digital Advertising Alliance).
  • Google Analytics: install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
  • Your browser: block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking advertising cookies may make ads less relevant but will not prevent you from using the site.

No personal health information

Please do not send us personal health information.

GLP1Bridge.com does not collect, request, or store personal health information. We are not a healthcare provider and are not subject to HIPAA. Our tools are educational and process your inputs only within your browser — they are never sent to us.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date shown at the top of this page. Changes take effect when posted.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, email us at hello@glp1bridge.com or visit our contact page.

Disclaimer: GLP1Bridge.com is an independent informational resource and is not affiliated with Medicare, CMS, or any drug manufacturer. This policy is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.
