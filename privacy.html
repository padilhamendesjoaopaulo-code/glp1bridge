Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GLP1Bridge.com ("we," "us") handles information when you visit our website. By using the site, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We do not require you to create an account, and our interactive tools (eligibility wizard, cost calculator, weight-projection calculator) run entirely in your browser. We do not collect, transmit, or store the answers you enter. If you email us, we receive the information you choose to send.

Like most websites, our servers and analytics may automatically log non-identifying technical data such as browser type, device, approximate region, and pages visited.

Cookies and advertising

We use third-party advertising, including Google AdSense, to support the site. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can also opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising at aboutads.info.

Children's privacy

This site is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking advertising cookies may make ads less relevant but will not prevent you from using the site.

Health information

Please do not send us personal health information.

We are not a healthcare provider and are not subject to HIPAA. Our tools are educational and do not store your inputs.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@glp1bridge.com or see our contact page.
